Comparison of the performance of Indonesian Tala and Nazhief Adriani stemming algorithms using python

Abstract

Stemming is the process of reducing the affixes from a word to become a basic word. Stemming is very useful in the process of indexing and searching in Information Retrieval (IR). The right stemming algorithm will improve IR performance. This study compares two stemming algorithms for Indonesian, namely the Tala algorithm andNazief & Adriani. We took 50 documents from the rumahginjal.id site and analyzed the terms contained in the document, then performed a stemming process for each term using the Tala andNazief & Adriani algorithms. The python programming languagethat have lots of library was used in this study and was supportedby flask (Python Web Framework) to display resultsin web-based application.Based on the research that has been done, it was found that the Tala algorithm has an average processing time of 0.000026 seconds with an accuracy of 41.63%. While Nazief Adriani has an average processing time of 0,000309 seconds with an accuracy of 61.23%. It can be concluded that the Tala algorithm is excellent in processing time while the Nazief & Adriani algorithm is excellent in the accuracy of stemming results.

Keyword: Stemming, Information Retrieval, Nazhief & Adriani, Tala
